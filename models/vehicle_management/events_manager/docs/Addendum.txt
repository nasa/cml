Addendum to verification:

With the addition of the option to specify actions as entities rather than as
executives in an extended class, a new sim was added -- SIM_verif_actions.

This sim has a very similar structure to that for SIM_verif and the resulting
data is very similar.

The difference lies in:


- RUN_sim_stop_var_adjust_logging_a:
  - In SIM_verif, the "log-now" occurs as part of the events specific-execution,
    then the complementary-changes take place, which include the assignment of
    base_bool to false. So in this run, the former value of base_bool is logged.
  - In SIM_verif_actions, the "log-now" occurs as part of an additional action,
    so occurs as part of the complementary-changes, and occurs AFTER the
    assignment of base_bool. So in this run, the new value of base_bool is
    logged.

- RUN_delayed_check
  - Has an extra log at t=1.8 because the "log-now" acction was added to this
    event.

- RUN_2_variable_check:
  - Two OR check forces a log at t=0.2, 0.3, 0.6, 0.7 that did not exist before
  - Two AND check terminates the sim at t=0.7, previously it went to t=1.
